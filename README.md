
This is the research group of computer architecture, communications and systems (ARCOS). Our group represents the Computer Architecture and Technology Area at the Carlos III of Madrid University.

Its main objective is to investigate and develop hardware and software systems in the areas of real-time and embedded systems, high-performance computing, storage systems, dependable systems.

Our group teaches several interesting lectures on Computer Architecture, Operating Systems, Distributed Systems, Real-Time Systems, Fault-Tolerant Systems in undergraduate and Master programs.
